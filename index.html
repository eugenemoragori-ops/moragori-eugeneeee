About Me

My name is Moragori Eugene. I am a focused and passionate researcher on natural remedies, traditional medicine, and plant-based healing solutions. My work is driven by a deep interest in understanding how nature provides safe, affordable, and effective remedies for common and chronic health conditions.

I study medicinal plants, herbs, roots, and natural formulations used in African traditional medicine and other global cultures. I am especially interested in evidence-based natural remedies, proper preparation methods, dosage safety, and how traditional knowledge can work alongside modern science.

Through continuous research, reading, experimentation, and documentation, I aim to educate people on responsible use of natural remedies while promoting sustainability, conservation of medicinal plants, and ethical knowledge sharing.

Research Focus & Skills

  • Traditional African natural remedies
  • Herbal preparation methods (infusions, decoctions, tinctures)
  • Natural immunity boosters
  • Documentation and knowledge sharing
  • Basic scientific research and observation
  • Ethical and safe use of natural medicine
